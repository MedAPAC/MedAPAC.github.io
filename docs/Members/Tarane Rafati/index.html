Biography

I am a computer engineer with a strong focus on computer vision and medical imaging, passionate about developing intelligent systems that bridge the gap between AI research and healthcare applications. My current work centers on explainable artificial intelligence (XAI), where I design and evaluate models that not only achieve high performance but also provide transparent, interpretable insights into their decision-making process. By combining technical expertise with a drive for impactful solutions, I aim to advance the reliability and trustworthiness of AI in clinical practice.
