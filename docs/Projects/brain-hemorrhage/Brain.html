Intracranial Hemorrhage AI

A comprehensive pipeline based on advanced algorithms for the analysis of non-contrast head CT scans. Our system provides rapid classification, segmentation, and quantification of intracranial hemorrhages and their suptypes to assist surgeons, radiologists, and emergency room's specialities in critical decision-making.

View Product Details Explore AI Modules

Core AI Modules

Classification & Subtyping

Detects the presence of hemorrhage and classifies it into subtypes (e.g., Intraparenchymal, Subdural) with high accuracy.

Detection & Tracking

Identifies and tracks the location and progression of hemorrhage lesions across multiple scans over time.

Segmentation & Volumetry

Precisely segments the hemorrhage area to calculate its volume, a critical biomarker for patient prognosis.

Explainable AI (XAI)

Provides visual heatmaps (e.g., Grad-CAM) to show which parts of the image the AI focused on for its predictions, ensuring transparency.

Vision-Language Models (VLMs)

Generates descriptive text reports from the CT scan images, summarizing findings in natural language for radiologists.
